ClusterEnG is an interactive platform for clustering analysis, developed by the Computational Biology Group at UIUC. It implements a number of mainstream clustering algorithms, such as K-means, DBSCAN, and spectral clustering. With this tool, you can play with different clustering algorithms, using either built-in datasets or your own data, to interpret the clustering results from the visualization. In this lesson, we will introduce the general usage of ClusterEnG, including its framework, how to select the datasets, how to choose different clustering algorithms, and how to interpret the results.

The ClusterEnG lesson is optional for this course.

ClusterEnG can be accessed via: http://education.knoweng.org/clustereng/index.php .
