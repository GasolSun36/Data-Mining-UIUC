Lesson 2: Efficient Pattern Mining Methods

Lesson Overview

In this lesson, we will learn the downward closure (or Apriori) property of frequent patterns and three major categories of methods for mining frequent patterns. First, we will introduce the most popular Apriori algorithm and understand why it is efficient in mining massive data sets. This is followed by some extensions of the Apriori algorithm. Second, we will introduce the second methodology, ECLAT, which explores vertical data format in mining. Third, we will introduce the third methodology, the pattern-growth approach, represented by FPGrowth. Finally, we will examine how to mine closed frequent patterns by introducing some ideas of Closet+.

Goals and Objectives

After you actively engage in the learning experiences in this module, you should be able to:

  • Understand the downward closure (or Apriori) property of frequent patterns and understand why it is critically important for efficient pattern mining.

  • Know some details of the Apriori algorithm and understand why it is efficient in mining massive transactional datasets.

  • Know some extensions of the Apriori algorithm under the same candidate-generation-and-test framework.

  • Understand that under some conditions, it is efficient to mine frequent patterns by exploring vertical data format.

  • Know some details of the FPGrowth algorithm and understand why pattern-growth approach is promising in frequent pattern mining.

  • Understand some basic optimization ideas to directly mine closed patterns.

Guiding Questions

Develop your answers to the following guiding questions while watching the video lectures throughout the lesson.

  • What is the downward closure property of frequent patterns? Why is this property critically important for efficient pattern mining?

  • What is the essence of the Apriori algorithm? Why is it efficient in mining massive transactional datasets?

  • How can the Apriori algorithms be further improved under the same candidate-generation-and-test framework?

  • What is the major idea of ECLAT? Under what conditions that it is efficient to mine frequent patterns by exploring vertical data format?

  • What is the major idea of FPGrowth in frequent pattern mining? Why is pattern-growth approach efficient?

  • Under what conditions that it is more advantageous to mine closed patterns directly instead of first mining all the frequent patterns?
